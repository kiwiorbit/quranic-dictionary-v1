1. Core Meaning

The basic meaning is to hear or to listen.

It can also extend to accepting or paying attention to something heard.

In religious contexts, it can imply obedience after hearing (hearing and then complying).


2. Common Forms Derived from س م ع

Arabic Meaning
سَمِيعَ He heard / listened
يَسْمَعُ He hears / is hearing / will hear
اِسْمَعْ Listen! (command)
سَمْعٌ Hearing (the sense of hearing)

3. Usage of Root Letters in Different Forms

Pattern Word Meaning & Explanation
فَعِيلٌ سَمِيعٌ Active participle meaning “one who hears” or “listener”; also one of the Names of Allah (The All-Hearing).
فَعَّالٌ سَمَّاعٌ Intensive form meaning “one who listens attentively” or “very attentive listener”.
مَفْعَلٌ مَسْمَعٌ A noun indicating “place of hearing” or “earshot”.
مَفْعُولٌ مَسْمُوعٌ Passive participle meaning “heard” or “audible”.

4. Qur’anic Meanings and References

  • 1 – To hear

    (24:16) وَلَوْلَا إِذْ سَمِعْتُمُوهُ قُلْتُمْ مَا يَكُونُ لَنَا أَنْ نَتَكَلَّمَ بِهَذَا

    — if only when you heard it you had said, ‘It is not [proper] for us to speak of this!’

    (2:171) مَا لاَ يَسْمَعُ إِلَّا دُعَاءً وَنِدَاءً

    — a dumb animal [lit. one who hears nothing but a shout and a cry]

  • 2 – To hear of

    (12:31) فَلَمَّا سَمِعَتْ بِمَكْرِهِنَّ أَرْسَلَتْ إِلَيْهِنَّ

    — so when she heard of their malicious talk, she sent for them

  • 3 – To have the faculty of hearing

    (19:42) لِمَ تَعْبُd مَا لاَ يَسْمَعُ وَلَا يُبْصِرُ

    — why do you worship something that can neither hear nor see?!

  • 4 – To listen

    (41:26) وَقَالَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا لَا تَسْمَعُوا لِهَذَا الْقُرْءَانِ

    — the disbelievers say, ‘Do not listen to this Qur’an’

  • 5 – To obey / listen to

    (36:25) إِنِّي آمَنتُ بِرَبِّكُمْ فَٱسْمَعُونِ

    — I believe in your Lord, so listen to me / obey me

  • 6 – To know / acknowledge

    (58:1) قَدْ سَمِعَ ٱللَّهُ قَوْلَ ٱلَّتِي تُجَادِلُكَ فِي زَوْجِهَا وَتَشْتَكِي إِلَى ٱللَّهِ

    — God has heard the words of the one who disputes with you concerning her husband and complains to God
